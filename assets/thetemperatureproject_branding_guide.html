Branding Guide: thetemperatureproject.com

Color Palette

  • Warm Red-Orange (Thermometer): #F45C2B
  • Gold-Orange Accent: #FDC239
  • Blue (Water Drop/Cloud): #32A8E0
  • Green (Bar Chart): #2CB67D
  • Teal (Pressure Gauge): #26A69A
  • Dark Text Gray: #2C3E50

Typography

Recommended Font: A modern sans-serif font such as Montserrat, Open Sans, or Roboto.

Logo Usage

Use the simplified icon for small sizes (e.g., favicon, mobile headers). Use the full logo with text for websites, presentations, or print.

Maintain adequate whitespace around the logo and avoid distortion or recoloring.
